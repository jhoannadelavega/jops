About me

"She believed she could, so she did!"

Hello! I'm Jhoanna, a second-year nursing student. Welcome to SN-Connect. I believe you're here for a reason, and don't worry, I'm here to help! This website will provide you with tips and advice related to nursing school, including possible classes to attend (majors and minors), nursing equipment for your return demonstrations, as well as community and clinical duties."

When I entered nursing school, I was not fully prepared. I initially thought that the nursing course would be easy, but during the first week, I must admit that I felt overwhelmed. As an average student, I realised that I needed to put in extra effort when it came to studying because the high school setup was no longer applicable. If you are an incoming first-year student, this website will help prepare you for the challenges of being a student nurse.
